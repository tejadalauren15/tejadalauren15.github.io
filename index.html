About

01 — Who I am

I'm a freelance graphic artist with experience designing visual materials for print and digital media, branding, social media, and e-commerce. I focus on producing clean, impactful designs that communicate ideas effectively while maintaining consistency and attention to detail.

My experience includes conceptualizing and producing custom designs for physical merchandise such as T-shirts, mugs, tarpaulins, invitations, and other promotional materials in digital printing. I create high-quality, print-ready artwork and ensure every design meets production standards.

I also specialize in logo design and brand identity, helping businesses establish memorable and cohesive visual identities. As a social media designer, I've developed creative content and Facebook advertising materials that strengthen brand presence and engage target audiences. In the e-commerce space, I've designed product listings, promotional banners, and campaign assets for platforms like Lazada, including Lorikeet campaign creatives, to enhance product presentation and support online sales.

I enjoy transforming ideas into compelling visual designs that help businesses communicate their message, build their brand, and connect with their audience across both digital and print platforms.
